# 🌿 Taichi WIKI

> **Open encyclopedia of Tai Chi Chuan — English**
>
> 🌐 **[Read in Vietnamese →](https://henryPhamDuc.github.io/taichi/)**

Open-source English encyclopedia of Tai Chi Chuan (Thái Cực Quyền), built with Material for MkDocs and deployed to GitHub Pages.

## 📚 What's inside

**148 articles** across 8 sections:

- 🌱 **Foundations** (3) — What Tai Chi Chuan is, where it came from
- 📜 **History & Philosophy** (6) — Wudang, Zhang Sanfeng, the major styles
- 🎯 **Ten Essentials & Principles** (27) — 10 essentials + core principles
- 💪 **Technique & Internal Power** (14) — Silk-reeling, Push Hands, Eight Gates
- 🌊 **Forms & Applications** (3) — Forms and martial applications
- 🧘 **Training & Drills** (72) — Standing meditation, daily drills
- 🌿 **Health & Longevity** (9) — Health cultivation, meditation, TCM
- 🎾 **Tennis Applications** (14) — Taichi applied to tennis

## 🕸️ Interactive Knowledge Graph

Every article is a node, every cross-link is an edge. Hover, click, drag, zoom.
