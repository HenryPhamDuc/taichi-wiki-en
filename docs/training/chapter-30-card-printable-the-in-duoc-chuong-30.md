    ╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
    ║  CHAPTER 30 CARD — THE TENSION PARADOX                     ║

    ║  🎯 ONE BIG IDEA / Ý TƯỞNG CỐT LÕI:                      ║
    ║     Soft is the path to strong. Iron inside, cotton out.  ║

    ║  • "Strong muscles = weak Taichi (at first)"              ║
